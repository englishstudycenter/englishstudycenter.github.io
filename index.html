A leader in ESL and academic preparation, English Study Center is located in the beautiful City of Osasco, São Paulo, Brazil. English Study Center has two streams of language training to choose from including general ESL classes focusing on spoken English and IELTS & TOEFL Exams. In class and online programs are available.

Our General English courses offer three levels of intensity, to help you reach your goals at a pace that suits you. We want you to reach your full potential by learning through doing, and immersing yourself in the most inspirational settings with like-minded people.

IELTS & TOEFL

The TOEFL test is the most respected test in the world, recognized by thousands of higher education institutions, universities and agencies in more than 130 countries, including Australia, Canada, the United Kingdom and the United States.
IELTS is the most popular certification in the world. Recognized and accepted in major European universities, IELTS is also requested in immigration proceedings for countries like Australia and Canada.
